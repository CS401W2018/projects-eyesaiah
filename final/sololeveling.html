Introduction

The Solo Leveling it is a south korean web novel written by Chu-Gong, The story takes place in world where portals to dangerous dungeons appear, and Hunters as individual with special powers are tasked to explore them.


Sung Jin-Woo, is a E-rank Hunter, it considered one of the weakest. After a near fatal encounter in a double dungeon, he unexpecdly gains the ability to level up like a video game character, becoming stronger with each task he completes.


The series shows couarage, loyalty, and resilence that proves to resolve to protect and fight for what is important.
