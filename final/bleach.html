Introduction

The Bleach is a popular japanese anime and manga series created by Tite Kubo.


The story of the Bleach the teenager name is Ichigo Kurosaki, who accidently acquires the powers of a Soul Reaper and being tasked with protecting the living from the evil spirits they call themselves as Hollows.


The series shows about friendship, loyalty, and the struggle between good and evil.

Tite Kubo The Author of Bleach

Born in June 26, 1977

Kubo began to draw in his young age and pursued a career in manga and he is known of his unique and often unconventional characters designs, as well as his ability to create memorable villians and plotlines.
