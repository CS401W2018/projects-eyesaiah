Introduction

The One Piece is a legendary anime and manga series created by Eiichiro Oda. It follow the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy, a young pirate with a dream of becoming the King of Pirates.


With his loyal crew, the Straw Hat Pirates, Luffy sails across the Grand Line in order to search of the ultimate treasure, The One Piece.


The series is known for it's rich world-building, epic battles, and deep story telling, making it one of the most beloved and the longest running series in anime history.
