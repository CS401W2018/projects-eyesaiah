Introduction

The My Hero Academia is a popular japanese anime and manga series created by the author of Kohei Horikoshi.


The world where nearly everyone possesses superpowers known as "Quirks" and there's a kid name Izuku Midoriya, a quirkless boy who dreams of becoming a hero like his idol, All Might.


Despite not having a quirk, Midoriya is granted the powerful ability of All Might and enrolled in U.A High School, where students are trained to become a professional heroes.

Kohei Horikoshi The Author of My Hero Academia

Born in November 20, 1986

His former father was a former manga artist and his mother worked as a teacher, He developed a passion for drawing at a young age and He is inspired by other manga artist
