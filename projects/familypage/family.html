Each Member Sakamoto Family Background:

Taro Sakamoto

Taro is a former world renowed assassin and member of the order and He is a highly skilled assassin, known for his combat abilities and strategic thinking.

Right now He lives a seemingly ordinary life, running a convenience store with his family, Aoi his wife and his daughter Hana.

After that the bounty had placed on his head, forcing him to once again embrace his past and return to the world of assassins, alongside his new allies Shin, Lu, and Heisuke.

Aoi Sakamoto

She is the wife of Taro Sakamoto and the mother of Hana Sakamoto. She was the reason why Taro Sakamoto abandoned his life as a assassin and showed him a better path to protecting his loved ones.

Hana Sakamoto

She is the daughter of Taro and Aoi Sakamoto, she is bright and kind personality having a cheerful and compassionate, disposotion, similar to her mother, Aoi.
