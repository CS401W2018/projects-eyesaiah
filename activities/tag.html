3. What are special entity characters?

- The special entity characters in the text that have special meaning in certain context such as HTML, XML ,or other markup langugaes.

These characters are often reserved because they are used to control formatting, structure, or behaviour.

4. What are phrase tags?

- They are HTML tags that used to control the meaning, style, or semantics of inline text within an HTML document.

They provide context to specific portions of text without altering its structure.

What is the difference between OL and UL Tags?

- OL it is called Ordered List, an HTML tag used to create a list of items where the order is meaningful, typically displayed with numbers or letters.

UL it is called Unordered List, an HTML tag used to create a list of items where the order does not matter, typically displayed with bullets.
