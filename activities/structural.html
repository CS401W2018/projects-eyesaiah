Structural Tags

- HEADER, NAV, MAIN, FOOTER

What goes in the header?

- Search Bar, Logo, and Company name

What goes in the Nav? Can the Nav go in the header? Does it have to go in the header? Does the Nav contain links to another website?

- The nav element typically contains a navigation links that helps the users move between different sections or pages of a website.

- The nav element can go in the header.

- The nav element does not have to go in the header.

- The nav can contain links to another website, but it depends on the purpose of the navigation.

What goes in Main?

- The main element is an semantic HTML tag that represents the main content of a webpage.

What goes in the Footer?

- The footer element is a semantic HTML tag that represents the footer of a webpage or a specific section.
