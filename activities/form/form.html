Questions:

1. The form tag has an "action" attribute. What does it do?

- Action attribute specifies the URL where the form data will be sent.

2. The form tag has a "method" attribute. What does it do?

- Method attribute specifies how the form data will be sent like GET or POST.

3. What is the difference between a "POST" and a "GET"?

- POST sends the data in the request body more secure, while GET sends the data in the URL in less secure.
